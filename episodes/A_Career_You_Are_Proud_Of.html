A Career You're Proud Of

Published: 08/01/2021

Our careers are an important part of our life. They aren't what wholly defines us as people, but they do play a very important part in that definition. When you look back, can you say that you are proud of the work, proud of the companies that you have been part of? It's a nostalgic and philosophical question to look back and think about how your career has shaped you as an individual. As you look forward in your, however, how are you being discerning of your next steps to ensure future work aligns with your goals and desires? In this episode, I talk about career progression, industries, and companies that are diametrically opposed to my values, and pose questions to you about how you want to look back at a career and what impact have you made?
