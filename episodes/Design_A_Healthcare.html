Design In Healthcare

Published: 01/06/2020

Is healthcare really poised to become more patient-centered? Shouldn't it already be focused on the patient? Let's take a look at a few topics around the patients experience and digital technology that promises sweeping changes to the industry. While healthcare is incredibly personal and private, we all have some shared experiences through the patient's journey that directly effect your health outcomes. Let's talk about how physicians and healthcare organizations operate and look at how designing health systems together, that we may create a paradigm shift and change in perspective with how we interact with healthcare systems.
