Our Uncomfortable Seat

Published: 09/12/2021

Designers have been clamoring to be taken more seriously and have that fabled "seat at the table." Guess what? We have our seats. The explosive growth of design is indicative of our maturation. But that seat seems a bit uncomfortable at times, doesn't it? Have we replaced discovery and thought with speed and over-operationalization? Have we lost our soul and identity to be part of other disciplines? It's tricky to discern, but this episode delves into our place at the table and how we can ensure our seat remains.
