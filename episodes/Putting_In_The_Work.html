Putting In The Work

Published: 07/19/2021

Denniz PoP: a prolific music producer behind some of the catchiest damn pop hits of the '90s and early 2000s. From Backstreet Boys, Britney Spears, Robyn, and my personal favorite, Ace of Base, Denniz PoP was a surprising hitmaker being based in Sweden. Watching Netflix's "This is Pop" docu-series has uncovered an interesting insight and correlation to work as a designer; the focus on craft and high standards we hold ourselves creates the benchmarks we measure against. In this episode, we delve into how dedication to craft and a focus on quality is more meaningful than constantly self-promoting oneself...that the best promotion is when others recognize your quality and see your dedication as their benchmark.
